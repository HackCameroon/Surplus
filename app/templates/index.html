About Sur+

Sur+ is an e-commerce platform built for local food services to market excess inventory, meal packages, or individual products. Due to COVID-19, many small businesses are losing revenue, and the food service industry is being hit the hardest. With Sur+, restaurants, private chefs, and certified food services can sell anything from their speciality ingriediants to at-home meal kits. For customers, your favorite restaurant cravings that COVID-19 disrupted can now be satisfied.
